Machine learning solution for getaround 🚗 🚙 🛻 🚐

👉 Here we help you calculate the best rental price per day for your car

In order to predict the best rental price per day for your car, we need a .json input format with the following information for your car:

  • model_key
  • mileage
  • engine_power
  • fuel
  • paint_color
  • car_type
  • private_parking_available
  • has_gps
  • has_air_conditioning
  • automatic_car
  • has_getaround_connect
  • has_speed_regulator
  • winter_tires

Send your request to the following url:

https://getaroundprice-app-amv.herokuapp.com/predict
